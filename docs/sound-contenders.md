# Sound Contenders for Alien Barrage

All files from: `AD Sounds/AudioSound FX/MobileArcadeSoundEffects/`

---

## playerShoot — Player fires a bullet
1. **SciFi/SFX_SciFiLaserShotFlat1.wav** — Clean flat laser, quick and punchy. Fires frequently so it needs to be short and non-fatiguing.
2. **SciFi/SFX_SciFiLaserShotFlat5.wav** — Variant with slightly different tone, good alternative.
3. **Bonus/SmallShotv1/SFX_SmallShotv1.wav** — Tiny shot sound, very short. Good for rapid fire.

## playerHit — Player takes damage (not dead)
1. **Bonus/GetHitv3/SFX_GetHitv3.wav** — Impact hit, clear feedback that something bad happened.
2. **Bonus/GetHitv1/SFX_GetHitv1.wav** — Different hit variant, punchier.
3. **Bonus/Hurtv1/SFX_Hurtv1.wav** — Hurt/damage sound, distinct from enemy death.

## playerDeath — Player loses last life
1. **Bonus/Explosionv1/SFX_Explosionv1.wav** — Explosion with weight, fits a dramatic final death.
2. **Explosion/SFX_Explosion3.wav** — Heavier explosion, more bass.
3. **Explosion/SFX_Explosion5.wav** — Another explosion variant, slightly different character.

## enemyDeath — Alien is destroyed
1. **Bonus/EnemyDiev1/SFX_EnemyDiev1.wav** — Purpose-built enemy death sound, should be short since many aliens die.
2. **Chiptune/SFX_ChipTuneDoDamageHit1.wav** — Retro chiptune hit, arcade feel.
3. **Chiptune/SFX_ChipTuneDoDamageHit2.wav** — Variant, slightly different pitch.

## enemyShoot — Enemy fires a bullet (currently unused in code but slot exists)
1. **SciFi/SFX_SciFiLaserShotFlat7.wav** — Different laser tone from player, avoids confusion.
2. **SciFi/SFX_SciFiLaserShotFlat9.wav** — Lower-pitched laser variant.
3. **Bonus/SmallShotv1/SFX_SmallShotv1variation01.wav** — Subtle shot variation.

## explosion — General explosion effect
1. **Explosion/SFX_Explosion1.wav** — Standard arcade explosion.
2. **Explosion/SFX_Explosion4.wav** — Slightly different character.
3. **Bonus/Explosionv1/SFX_Explosionv1variation01.wav** — Variation of the bonus explosion.

## powerupCollect — Player picks up a powerup
1. **Bonus/PowerUpv1/SFX_PowerUpv1.wav** — Classic powerup collection sound.
2. **Bonus/PowerUpv2/SFX_PowerUpv2.wav** — Alternative powerup, different tone.
3. **Bonus/CollectCoinv1/SFX_CollectCoinv1.wav** — Coin collect sound, works for pickup feedback.

## powerupExpire — Powerup timer runs out
1. **Bonus/SlideDownv1/SFX_SlideDownv1.wav** — Descending slide, conveys "wearing off."
2. **Bonus/SlideDownv3/SFX_SlideDownv3.wav** — Another descending variant.
3. **Chiptune/SFX_ChipTuneTonalWrongFeeling1.wav** — Subtle "wrong" tone, signals loss of buff.

## ufoAppear — UFO enters the screen
1. **SciFi/SFX_SciFiEngine1.wav** — Sci-fi engine hum, signals incoming UFO.
2. **Whoosh/SFX_WhooshSciFiPassByShip1.wav** — Sci-fi flyby whoosh.
3. **SciFi/SFX_SciFiEnginePitchDown1.wav** — Engine pitch-down, eerie arrival feel.

## ufoDestroyed — UFO is shot down
1. **Explosion/SFX_Explosion7.wav** — Distinct explosion for the big bonus kill.
2. **Explosion/SFX_Explosion8.wav** — Heavier explosion variant.
3. **Bonus/Explosionv1/SFX_Explosionv1variation02.wav** — Variation with different tail.

## levelStart — New level begins
1. **Bonus/StageClearv1/SFX_StageClearv1.wav** — Stage clear / level start fanfare.
2. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareDrums1.wav** — Drum fanfare, energizing.
3. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareDrums3.wav** — Another drum fanfare variant.

## gameOver — Game over screen
1. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareMusicLose1.wav** — Lose fanfare, somber but game-appropriate.
2. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareMusicLose3.wav** — Different lose music variant.
3. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareMusicLose5.wav** — Third lose variant, pick the best tone.

## menuSelect — UI button press in menus
1. **Bonus/UIOptionChangev1/SFX_UiOptionChangev1.wav** — Clean UI option change sound.
2. **UI/SFX_UIClickFastHigh1.wav** — Fast high click, snappy.
3. **UI/SFX_UIRetroChiptune1.wav** — Retro chiptune click, matches arcade aesthetic.

## extraLife — Player gains an extra life
1. **Bonus/PowerUpv3/SFX_PowerUpv3.wav** — Distinct powerup sound, more celebratory.
2. **Reward/SFX_RewardMusical1.wav** — Musical reward, signals something special.
3. **UI/SFX_UIConfirmMusicalHappy1.wav** — Happy confirmation, positive feedback.

## highScore — New high score achieved
1. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareMusicWin1.wav** — Win fanfare, celebratory.
2. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareMusicWin3.wav** — Alternative win fanfare.
3. **Fanfare/SFX_FanfareMusicWinCrowd1.wav** — Win with crowd, most dramatic.
